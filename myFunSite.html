Featured Trip

During the Winter, Leavenworth becomes one of the most popular locations in Washington State. Our users suggest staying there for about a week to truly enjoy everything the town has to offer. You can view all the lights set up around the town as well as go skiing nearby. There are many museums and even wine breweries for you to visit without spending an excessive amount of money. There are other nearby cities to explore too. Click on the links in the navigation bar to explore more.

View user stories

With over 200 users uploading posts from their trips to Leavenworth, you can find even more suggestions for ideas on what you can do while visiting the town.
